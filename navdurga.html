Collection

Navdurga

A Navratri collection of thirteen original yantras — drawn during the nine sacred nights of the Divine Mother, honouring Shakti in all her forms.

Created in the spirit of Navratri — the nine sacred nights of the Divine Mother — this collection is a meditative offering to Shakti in all her forms. Each yantra was drawn by hand with archival ink on 400 GSM cold-pressed paper during the auspicious days of the festival, when the veil between the material and the divine is at its thinnest. The collection moves through the nine manifestations of Durga and extends into the chakra yantras and sacred geometries that map consciousness itself — from the grounding earth of the Muladhara to the infinite stillness of the Sahasrara. Together, these thirteen works form a single devotional journey: an artist's prayer rendered in ink, geometry, and colour.
